# LOCAL RALPH ROUND 04

## Top issue identified
Mission impact was implicit; players needed a concise, in-game explanation of why each turn affects real emergency outcomes.

## Focused improvement implemented
Added a persistent **Why this matters** panel tied to CareRoute mission:
- pre-run: explains end-to-end CareRoute purpose
- per-turn: shows one plain-language impact sentence for current stage
- end-run: reinforces cross-stakeholder mission impact and patient pathway summary

## Mobile-friendliness check
Uses existing card-style text blocks; no additional controls introduced.

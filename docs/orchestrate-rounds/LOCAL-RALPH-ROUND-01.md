# LOCAL RALPH ROUND 01

## Top issue identified
Players could not clearly tell whose decision each turn represented, so role-play felt cosmetic.

## Focused improvement implemented
Added explicit stage ownership and objective text on every turn:
- each stage now has `owner` + `objective`
- stage card now shows **Decision Owner** and **Turn Objective**
- coach text now clarifies whether this is your decision turn or another stakeholder’s turn
- history now records the stakeholder who made each choice

## Mobile-friendliness check
No new controls added; large tap buttons retained.

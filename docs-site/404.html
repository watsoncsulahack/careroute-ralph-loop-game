How CareRoute Works

1) Intake

Capture condition, acuity, and location.

2) Match

Score ambulance + hospital + insurance options.

3) Recommend

Select best route by time, fit, and cost signals.

4) Dispatch

Coordinate pickup and destination with traceability.

Why Teams Use CareRoute

  • Faster pickup and arrival decisions
  • Improved facility matching for patient needs
  • Lower avoidable patient cost exposure
  • Clear decision trace for governance and review
